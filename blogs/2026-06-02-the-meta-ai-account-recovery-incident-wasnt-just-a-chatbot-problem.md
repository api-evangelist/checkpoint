Hackers reportedly used Meta's AI support chatbot to gain access to high-profile Instagram accounts by requesting email address changes, affecting accounts linked to the Obama White House, Sephora, and the Chief Master Sergeant of the Space Force. Check Point argues the incident reveals a deeper authorization and system design problem: the core vulnerability was not that the AI was tricked, but that it was positioned within a sensitive account recovery workflow with insufficient verification controls. The post calls for stricter guardrails around AI agents placed in security-sensitive workflows.
