As Amazon Prime Day approaches June 23-26, 2026, spanning over 25 countries, the shopping event becomes a target for cybercriminals. Phishing emails, fake websites, fraudulent offers, smishing campaigns, and account takeover attempts impersonating Amazon all surge during major retail moments that combine trusted brands, time-sensitive urgency, and large-scale purchasing activity.
