As the U.S. approaches the November 2026 midterm elections, the primary cybersecurity threat centers on information manipulation rather than direct voting system attacks. Russian-linked groups have cloned major news outlets including Reuters, The Washington Post, and Fox News using lookalike domains, approximately 4,010 domains containing "vote" were registered in a single month in early 2026, and around 9,500 leaked credentials from ActBlue and 6,500 from WinRed were discovered in criminal markets. Security teams should prioritize phishing prevention, brand protection monitoring, and leaked credential tracking as election season approaches.
