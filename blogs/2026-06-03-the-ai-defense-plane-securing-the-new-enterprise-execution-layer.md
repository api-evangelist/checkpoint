AI has become a new execution layer across enterprises where natural language can now influence system behavior, data access, and actions, requiring organizations to move beyond fragmented point controls. Check Point proposes a unified AI Defense Plane that coordinates discovery, protection, governance, and assurance across employee AI usage, embedded AI applications, and autonomous agents. The post argues that traditional security architectures are insufficient for a world where AI is no longer confined to a single application boundary.
