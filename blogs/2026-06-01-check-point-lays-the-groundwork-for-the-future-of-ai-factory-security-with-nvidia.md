At GTC Taipei during COMPUTEX 2026, Check Point and NVIDIA announced collaboration to establish infrastructure-native security for AI environments using NVIDIA's BlueField-4 STX architecture and DOCA framework. The partnership leverages BlueField data processing units to provide visibility, isolation, runtime protections, and policy enforcement within high-performance AI infrastructure, enabling Check Point's AI Factory Firewall to protect east-west traffic, multi-tenant deployments, and Kubernetes clusters. The integration addresses the security gaps created as organizations scale distributed training systems, inference pipelines, and increasingly autonomous AI agent operations.
