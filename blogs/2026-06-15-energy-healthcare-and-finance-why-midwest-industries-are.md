While national attack rates remained relatively flat year-over-year through May 2026, Central US organizations experienced elevated threat activity. Midwest companies faced about 1,552 cyber attacks per week, roughly 7% above the national average, with particular vulnerability in energy, healthcare, and financial sectors.
