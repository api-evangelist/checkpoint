A threat actor constructed an ecosystem across WordPress, GitHub, SourceForge, YouTube, and forums designed to manufacture trust in malicious tools. The research reveals how reputation systems themselves are now a target, with actors manipulating community votes and comments on security platforms to mislabel dangerous files as harmless.
